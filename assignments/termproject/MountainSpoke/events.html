UPCOMING EVENT - Cycle the Continental Divide

The event of the year is back!! Come join us for the Cycle the Continental Divide event. This will have tours for the whole family, regardless of what skill levels you have. Join any of the competition races, or just come out to support your fellow riders. There will be over 200 vendors at this event showing you some of the latest in cycling gear. So grab your significant other, kids, parents, grandparents, neighbors, or anyone who is a fellow rider, and head on down for the greatest event of the year.

Event Details

Dates: 27-29 July 2018

Routes: Various routes throughout the town.

Skill Level Required: Beginner - Expert

Location: Bozeman town square

Cost: $10 per race (free to enjoy the event)
