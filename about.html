WHAT WE DO

Texas Traffic Data is a traffic data consulting firm, performing a variety of traffic studies supporting the public and private sector. We provide accurate volume counts on all types of roadways, from small residential streets, to high volume interstate roadways.


Why Choose Us

We realize that transportation professionals need accurate data to successfully design and complete any project. All data is checked for accuracy by the quality control managers for approval before being submitted. Our hope is that we are able to better our communities one survey at a time.
